Press Bio

The Artist is a Johannesburg-born rapper, photographer, and multidisciplinary street creative carving his own lane at the intersection of South African hip-hop and visual culture. Raised on the concrete rhythms of Jozi, his music is as much architecture as it is sound — each track a deliberate frame, each project a world built from the ground up.

His debut Concrete EP announced an artist uninterested in template thinking — raw, considered, street-rooted but never confined to it. The follow-up Motion Tape expanded the universe, blending photography, fashion, and verse into something that resists genre. His latest EP, Artist × JHB, is the most complete statement yet: six tracks that document a city, a generation, and a creative refusing to wait for permission.
